QUALITY CONTROL AND ASSURANCE

We source raw materials from our mines with a commitment to purity and excellence, refining them through a rigorous process that includes water washing, fine slurry sifting, and other precise manufacturing methods. To ensure our global operations meet these exacting standards, we regularly send a specialised team to oversee and reinforce quality across all our sites.
